About Us

Agile, new-age and future-ready

Part of the USD 165 billion Tata Group, Tata Motors, a USD 44 billion organisation, is a leading global automobile manufacturer of cars, utility vehicles, pick-ups, trucks and buses. We are India’s market leader in commercial vehicles and amongst the top three in the passenger vehicles market. We prioritise human centricity with technological prowess and engineering excellence to make cargo and passenger mobility safer, smarter and greener.
